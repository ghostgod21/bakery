About Bakers.in

Bakers.in is a colourful, multi-category food storefront — cakes, pastries & desserts, pizza, burgers, mocktails, and cookies & snacks, all from one kitchen. This demo build ships with sample products and a full cart-to-checkout flow, ready to be adapted into a real bakery or cloud-kitchen ordering site.

Product photos here are colour-coded placeholders labelled with each product's name, so the app runs out of the box with zero setup and no image licensing risk. Swap them for real photography whenever you're ready to go live.

Orders placed here are stored for reference, and the selected payment method is captured as metadata only — no real payment is processed.
